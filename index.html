Mouse Speed Click (left): Keep clicking the left mouse button within the designated area until the timer completes. The countdown starts when you initiate the first click!

Mouse Speed Click (right): Keep clicking the right mouse button within the designated area until the timer completes. The countdown starts when you initiate the first click!

Fast Mouse Hovering: Keep hovering over the circle until the countdown is completed. The countdown starts when you hover over the circle for the first time!

Fast Clicking while Hovering: Continuously hover over the circle and click on it rapidly until the countdown is completed. The countdown begins when you first hover over the circle!

This website will allow you to practice and improve your skills in: mouse speed clicking, fast mouse hovering, and fast clicking while hovering, since it is necessary to be fast for gaming.

Instructions:

1. Set Time (in minutes).

2. Choose the test that you want to do.

3. The chronometer starts when you hover or click for the first time.
